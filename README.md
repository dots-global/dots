# DOTS
DOTS (stands for Data, Optimization, Technology & Strategy) is a data analytics and business intelligence firm focused on turning data into clear, actionable insights.
We help organizations understand performance, identify opportunities, and support better decision-making through analytics, reporting, and data-driven intelligence.
Our work spans business analytics, operational analytics, dashboards, and performance reporting, enabling teams to move from raw data to meaningful outcomes.
At DOTS, we believe analytics should bring clarity, structure, and direction not complexity. Our approach combines data expertise with practical business understanding to deliver insights that actually matter.
